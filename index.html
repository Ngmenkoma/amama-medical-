Why Choose Us
Healthcare Reimagined

At Amama Medical Centre, we bring together modern medical technology and compassionate care to serve patients of all ages. Whether you need a quick consultation or complex surgery, our experienced team is ready.

Located in Napogbakole Ext., Wa — adjacent to St. Benedicts Parish — we are your community's most trusted health partner.

  • Experienced and caring medical professionals
  • State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment
  • Comprehensive maternal & child health programs
  • Home care services available
  • Travel medicine and outreach programs
  • 24/7 emergency availability
